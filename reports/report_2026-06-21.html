The DOJ simultaneously closed its parallel criminal probe, providing significant legal closure for BEN shareholders. The $100M penalty, while material, was largely anticipated by the market following prior disclosures, and the DOJ declination removes the most severe tail risk — potential criminal enterprise charges. BEN shares remain under pressure year-to-date given broader active management industry headwinds, and the reputational damage to WAMCO's institutional client relationships remains an ongoing concern, particularly for defined benefit pension mandates. Current Status: Settled — civil penalty agreed, DOJ probe closed. Financial Exposure: $100M civil penalty confirmed; potential ongoing civil litigation from harmed clients not yet quantified.
